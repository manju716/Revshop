RevShop

RevShop is a simple console-based e-commerce application implemented in Java. It supports buyers and sellers, allowing users to register, login, browse products, place orders, and manage inventory. Sellers can manage products, view orders, and see product reviews.

Features Buyer Features

Register & login with email & password

Browse products by category / search

View product details: price, description, ratings, reviews

Add/remove products to/from cart

Manage cart (view and update quantities)

Place orders

Review and rate purchased products

View order notifications (simulated)

Change password, forgot password recovery (security question & hint)

Seller Features

Register with business details

Login with email & password

Add new products with description, MRP, discounted price, and category

Update inventory and set stock thresholds

Receive low stock alerts

View all orders for their products

View product reviews and ratings

Change password, forgot password recovery (security question & hint)
